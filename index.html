Wordle

Guess the five letter word in six tries!

After you enter your input, the program will give you some hints. It will return your word but each letter entered will have a symbol next to it.

An asterik * next to a word means that the letter is in the word of the day and guessed in the correct spot.

A hashtag # next to a word means that the letter is in the word but guessed in the wrong spot.

An exclamation point ! next to a word means that hte letter is not in the word at all.
